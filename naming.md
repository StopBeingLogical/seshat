# Naming Conventions & Active Names

## Naming Pillars

Names are chosen based on the asset's domain for immediate categorical recognition.

| Domain | Cultural Root | Purpose |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Hardware** | Greek / Latin | Physical nodes and infrastructure |
| **Software** | Sumerian | Codebases, scripts, and standalone applications |
| **Writing / AI Context** | Egyptian | Creative works, world-building, journals, and AI seed documents |

### Global Rules
- Systems are named for the **role they inhabit**, not their hardware specs
- Names persist across hardware changes
- No unique names for ephemeral layers — these use derived IDs
